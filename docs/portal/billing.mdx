The Billing page lets you view your current plan, upgrade or downgrade, and manage your payment method.

## Plans

ASO++ offers three plans:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Free" icon="hand-wave">
    **50 calls/month**

    5 keywords tracked

    Up to 3 tokens

    No credit card required

  </Card>
  <Card title="Starter" icon="rocket">
    **More calls/month**

    20 keywords tracked

    Up to 10 tokens

    Monthly or annual billing

  </Card>
  <Card title="Pro" icon="star">
    **Highest quota**

    50 keywords tracked

    Unlimited tokens

    Monthly or annual billing

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Exact call quotas and prices for Starter and Pro are shown on the Billing page
  in the portal. They may change during early access.
</Note>

## Upgrading your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Billing page">
    Click **Billing** in the sidebar.
  </Step>

<Step title="Choose a plan">
  Click **Upgrade** next to the plan you want. You'll be taken to a secure
  checkout.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter payment details">
  ASO++ uses [Polar.sh](https://polar.sh) for billing. Enter your card details
  and confirm the subscription.
</Step>

  <Step title="Confirm activation">
    After checkout, you're redirected back to the Billing page. Your new plan is active immediately — quota and token limits update right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downgrading your plan

To move to a lower plan, click **Change plan** on the Billing page and select the plan you want. The downgrade takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Until then, you keep your current plan's limits.

<Warning>
  If downgrading would put you over the new plan's keyword tracking limit,
  you'll need to remove keywords from the [Usage](/portal/usage) page first. The
  portal will warn you if this is the case before confirming the downgrade.
</Warning>

## Cancelling

To cancel your subscription, click **Cancel plan** on the Billing page. Your current plan stays active until the end of the billing period, then drops to the Free tier automatically. No data is deleted.

## Invoices

Past invoices are listed at the bottom of the Billing page. Click **Download** next to any invoice to get the PDF.

## Updating your payment method

Click **Update payment method** to replace the card on file. You'll be taken to the Polar.sh payment portal.

## Billing questions

For billing issues — duplicate charges, refunds, or VAT receipts — email [support@asopp.cc](mailto:support@asopp.cc) with your account email and a description of the issue.
